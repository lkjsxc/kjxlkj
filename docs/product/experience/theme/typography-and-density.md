# Typography and Density Contract

## Typography Roles

- Brand, page headings, and card titles use a clear sans family.
- Body copy and rendered Markdown use a readable text family with strong dark-mode legibility.
- Slugs, timestamps, and code-like metadata use monospace.

## Hierarchy

- Page titles are large and compact, with minimal extra whitespace.
- Section labels are small uppercase or label-style markers.
- Metadata remains secondary but stays readable at a glance.

## Density

- The product favors dense knowledge-workbench spacing over airy editorial spacing.
- Navigation cards, history entries, and dashboard rows use compact padding.
- Desktop content avoids wasteful empty space while preserving reading comfort.
- Mobile spacing tightens further without creating tap-target failures.

## Text Handling

- Long note titles wrap cleanly.
- Slugs and dates truncate only when absolutely necessary.
- Compact mode prioritizes title readability over decorative chrome.
